About the Show

60+ conversations. One question behind every one: how did you really build this?

I'm Deepa — Bay Area-based host, product manager, and lifelong creative. Built by Grit goes behind the art to ask the questions most interviews skip: what does this pay, what did it cost, and what kept you going?

Guests span continents — from NYT bestselling authors to visual artists to performers who've built careers on nothing but talent and tenacity.
