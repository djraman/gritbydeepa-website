About the Show

60+ conversations. One question behind every one: how did you really build this?

Built by Grit goes behind the art to ask the questions most interviews skip: what does this pay, what did it cost, and what kept you going? Guests span continents — from published authors to visual artists to performers who've built careers on nothing but talent and tenacity.

About the Host

I'm Deepa Janakiraman.

Bay Area-based product manager, lifelong creative, and host of Built by Grit. I've spent years inside the Indian classical arts world — as a hobbyist musician and community organizer — and I've been fascinated by one question: how do creatives actually build careers that last?

Built by Grit is my attempt to answer that question — honestly, globally, and without the highlight reel. Every guest gets asked the same three questions: what does this pay, what are your income streams, and what financial decision would you make differently?
